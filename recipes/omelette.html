How to make an omelette: a short recipe

Description

Welcome! I'll be showing you how to make an omelette in 4 easy steps

  1. Get the stuff ready

    2. You'll need the follow ingredients:

    • Egg
    • Salt
    • Powdered Pepper
    • Oil

    You can keep some chilli powder as well, if you like to have a spicy omelette.

    Also, keep a pan, a spoon or a stick to flip the omelette, and a plate.

  2. Step 2: Pour Oil Onto The Pan

    3. You will need to pour oil onto the pan after heating the pan for a few minutes.

    Note: Don't forget to wash your utensils before using them!

  3. Egg and ingredients

    4. Crack open an egg and put it in the hot pan. Then, add the ingredients one after another.

    The order I would recommend is, first sprinkle a pinch of salt evenly, then some pepper powder, and finally a bit of chilli powder if you to have it spicy.

  4. Close The Pan, Wait, And Voila!

    5. After adding all these ingredients, close the pan with plate.

    Wait for a minute or two, then, flip the egg if you prefer to have it cooked well.

    Again, close the pan if you have flipped it so that other side is cooked as well.

    After that, move the egg to a plate to eat.

There you go! Making an omelette wasn't so hard, was it?
